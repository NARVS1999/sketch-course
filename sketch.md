# 🎨 Sketch Technique Specialization Courses Guide

*A comprehensive 5-phase roadmap from beginner to advanced sketching.*

---

## 📌 PHASE 1: FOUNDATION & LINE CONTROL  
*"Bago ka magpa-fly, dapat marunong ka munang lumakad."*

**Goal:** Sanayin ang kamay, mata, at utak sa basic lines at shapes.

| Lesson | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | The Warm-Up Scribble | 5-minute non-stop doodle gamit ang iba't ibang pressure (light to heavy). Para mawala ang kaba sa kamay. |
| 2 | Straight Lines & Curves | Gumuhit ng parallel lines, cross-hatching, at S-curves nang hindi gumagamit ng ruler. Focus sa consistency. |
| 3 | Basic Geometric Shapes | Circle, square, triangle, at ellipses. Ulit-ulitin hanggang maging confident ang stroke mo. |
| 4 | Contour Drawing | Gumuhit ng outline ng isang bagay (e.g., coffee mug) nang hindi tinitignan ang papel (blind contour). Para mapraktis ang observation skills. |

---

## 📌 PHASE 2: SHADING & VOLUME  
*"Ang lihim ng realism ay nasa ilaw at anino."*

**Goal:** Matutong magbigay ng depth at form gamit ang shading techniques.

| Lesson | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| 5 | Light Source Identification | Alamin kung saan galing ang ilaw. Magdrawing ng sphere at ituro kung nasaan ang highlight, mid-tone, core shadow, at cast shadow. |
| 6 | Hatching & Cross-Hatching | Gumamit ng parallel lines at intersecting lines para gumawa ng gradient mula sa light hanggang dark. |
| 7 | Blending & Smudging | Gamit ang tissue, blending stump, o kahit daliri, mag-practice ng smooth shading sa mga prutas (e.g., apple, saging). |
| 8 | Texture Creation | Gumuhit ng iba't ibang surface: buhok (fur), makinis na metal, at magaspang na kahoy gamit ang iba't ibang stroke. |

---

## 📌 PHASE 3: PERSPECTIVE & PROPORTION  
*"Para maging realistic, kailangan tama ang sukat at anggulo."*

**Goal:** Matutong mag-drawing ng 3D objects at scenes na may tamang proportion.

| Lesson | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| 9 | 1-Point Perspective | Gumuhit ng kalsada o hallway na paliit nang paliit papunta sa iisang vanishing point. |
| 10 | 2-Point Perspective | Gumuhit ng building o cube mula sa corner view. Focus sa pag-align ng vertical at horizontal lines. |
| 11 | Sight-Size Method | Gamit ang pencil, i-measure ang proportion ng isang still life object para ma-transfer sa papel ng exact size. |
| 12 | Foreshortening | Gumuhit ng braso o kamay na nakatutok sa viewer. Para matutunan ang illusion ng lalim. |

---

## 📌 PHASE 4: PORTRAIT & ANATOMY  
*"Ang pinakamahirap pero pinaka-rewarding na subject—ang tao."*

**Goal:** Matutunan ang pagguhit ng mukha at katawan ng tao.

| Lesson | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| 13 | Facial Proportions | Gamit ang Loomis method, i-sketch ang skull shape at ilagay ang mga guidelines para sa mata, ilong, at bibig. |
| 14 | Features Study (Eyes & Nose) | Mag-focus sa pagdrawing ng mata na may realistic na iris at reflection, at ilong na may tamang shading sa ilalim. |
| 15 | Features Study (Lips & Ears) | Pag-aralan ang lip mapping (upper at lower lip) at ang curves ng ears. |
| 16 | Basic Figure Gesture | Gumuhit ng action poses gamit ang quick 30-second sketches (stick figures + basic muscles). Para makuha ang "movement" ng katawan. |

---

## 📌 PHASE 5: ADVANCED COMPOSITION & STYLE  
*"Hindi lang basta kopya; dapat may sarili kang signature."*

**Goal:** Matutong mag-compose ng sariling likha at mag-develop ng personal na style.

| Lesson | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| 17 | Rule of Thirds & Focal Point | Magplano ng drawing gamit ang grid (3x3) at pumili kung saan nakafocus ang mata ng viewer. |
| 18 | Negative Space Drawing | Sa halip na iguhit ang object, iguhit ang puwang sa paligid nito. Para ma-train ang spatial awareness. |
| 19 | Experimental Mark-Making | Gumamit ng iba't ibang medium (charcoal, ink, watercolor pencil) at i-explore ang expressive strokes (e.g., stippling, scumbling). |
| 20 | Final Masterpiece (Portfolio Piece) | Pagsamahin ang lahat ng natutunan sa isang detailed drawing (landscape, portrait, o imaginative scene). Ito ang magiging "bida" ng iyong sketch portfolio. |

---

## 🧰 Recommended Materials

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Sketchpad | At least A4 size, 100+ gsm |
| Graphite Pencils | Set ng 2H, HB, 2B, 4B, 6B, 8B |
| Eraser | Kneaded eraser + regular vinyl eraser |
| Blending Tools | Blending stump, tissue, o cotton buds |
| References | Still life objects o printed photos |
| Optional | Charcoal, ink pen, watercolor pencils |

---

## 🗓️ Suggested Study Schedule

| Phase | Duration (if daily) |
|-------|----------------------|
| Phase 1 | 1 week (4 days lessons + 3 days practice) |
| Phase 2 | 1.5 weeks |
| Phase 3 | 1.5 weeks |
| Phase 4 | 2 weeks |
| Phase 5 | 2 weeks (including final artwork) |

> **Total:** ~8 weeks ng consistent practice (1–2 hours per day).

---

## ✨ Tips for Success

- ✅ **Warm-up muna** – 5–10 mins of scribbles before every session.
- ✅ **Quantity over quality** sa simula – mas mahalaga ang dami ng practice.
- ✅ **Use references** – Huwag mahihiyang gumamit ng photos o real objects.
- ✅ **Record your progress** – I-save ang bawat output para makita ang improvement.
- ✅ **Join art communities** – Para sa feedback at inspiration.

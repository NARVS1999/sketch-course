# Mission: Learn Sketching for Personal Enjoyment

## Why
I want to develop sketching as a creative outlet — a relaxing, fulfilling hobby I can do anywhere. The goal is to get good enough that drawing feels natural and enjoyable, not frustrating.

## Success looks like
- I can sketch everyday objects, scenes, and people from observation with reasonable accuracy
- I can use shading, perspective, and composition to make drawings look dimensional and interesting
- I have a personal sketchbook habit I genuinely enjoy maintaining
- I can produce a final portfolio piece I'm proud to share

## Constraints
- 1–2 hours per day for practice
- Minimal budget — pencil, paper, eraser, basic blending tools
- No formal art classes; self-taught via structured practice

## Out of scope
- Digital art / tablet drawing (focus on traditional pencil sketching)
- Color theory and painting (graphite only for now)
- Advanced anatomy for medical illustration
- Hyper-realism as an end goal

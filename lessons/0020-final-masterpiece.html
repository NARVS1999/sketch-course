Lesson 0020: Final Masterpiece

Bring everything together — your portfolio piece.

Phase 5: Advanced Composition & Style

Lesson Goal Create one detailed drawing that combines all the skills from the course — line control, shading, perspective, proportion, and composition — as your portfolio piece.

Your Final Challenge

This is the capstone of the entire course. You'll create a single, detailed drawing that demonstrates everything you've learned. Choose one of three paths:

Path A: Landscape

Draw a landscape with depth: a foreground (detailed), midground (moderate detail), and background (soft/simple). Use 1-point or 2-point perspective for roads or buildings. Shade with a mix of hatching and blending. Apply the rule of thirds to place your focal point.

Path B: Portrait

Draw a portrait from a reference photo. Use the Loomis method for construction. Pay special attention to the eyes (catchlight), nose (shadow under), and lips (value contrast). Use smooth blending for skin and hatching for hair texture.

Path C: Imaginative Scene

Draw a scene from your imagination. It must include: at least one object with correct perspective, a figure or creature, shading with at least two techniques, and a composition planned using the rule of thirds.

Planning Your Masterpiece

1

Choose your path (A, B, or C). Gather reference materials if needed.

2

Plan the composition with thumbnail sketches. Try 3 different layouts before picking one.

3

Lightly sketch the basic construction (shapes, perspective lines, guidelines).

4

Add detail and shading. Work from light to dark. Save highlights for last.

5

Step back, evaluate, and refine. Sign your work!

What to Submit

  • Your final drawing (photo or scan)
  • A brief note on: which path you chose, what skills you used, what you're proudest of
  • Your thumbnail sketches (shows your planning process)
Remember This isn't about perfection. It's about showing how far you've come. Compare this drawing to your very first warm-up scribble from Lesson 1. The difference will amaze you.
